Our Story

OstelHub started in 2025 when five backpackers found themselves in the same cramped dorm in Jotsoma. They couldn't find their perfect hostel for themselves.

They were frustrated with fake reviews and high hostel fees without proper needs. They couldn't find their perfect hostel with their desire environment.

So we built something better — a trusted webpage where students can find their desire hostel according to their needs. We can check the hostel from our own comfort space or from our own homes without physically being there.

No ads. No commissions. Find your perfect stay.

🏠 Real Places

Every hostel is selected based on real experiences, not paid promotions.
