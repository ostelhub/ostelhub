Our Story

Hostel Finder started in 2025 when five backpackers with dreams found themselves in the same cramped dorm in Jotsoma.

They were frustrated with fake reviews and paid listings hiding genuinely great hostels.

So we built something better — a curated, honest directory based purely on real experiences.

No ads. No commissions. Just the best stays.
